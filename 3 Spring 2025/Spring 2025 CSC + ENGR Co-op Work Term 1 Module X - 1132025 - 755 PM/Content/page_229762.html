UPass Opt-in:

Students not enrolled in an on-campus course (such as when you are on a co-op work term) are not automatically enrolled in the UPass bus pass.  If you are on a co-op work term in Victoria, you can choose to opt-in to the U-Pass (Umo App/App).  

Please use the new U-Pass (Umo) online payment form, which can be found here.

The U-Pass (Umo) is administered by the UVic Student Society (UVSS) and not by Co-op.  Questions need to go to the UVSS Info Booth.  Its hours are currently Mon - Fri from 9am – 4pm.  For more information on the U-Pass, please visit  https://www.uvic.ca/students/campus-services/transportation-parking/student-bus-pass/index.php

  • As of this year, the University is no longer accepting part-time Athletics fees, so co-op students must now pay the full-time rate. The total amount that will be charged is $177.20 ($81 U-Pass (Umo) fee + $96.20 ATRS fee).

  • The UVSS can also accept payment in person (cash, debit and credit) and over the phone (credit only), but they are encouraging students to use the online form if possible as it is the most efficient and traceable payment method. The fees are not pro-rated, and remain the same regardless of how late the student chooses to opt into the package.

  • The Info Booth’s hours are currently Mon - Fri from 9am-4pm.

Regardless of how students pay for their U-Pass (Umo App/Card), they will still need to re-activate their UVic ONEcards in one of the U-Pass kiosks located on campus (one in the SUB beside the UVSS Info Booth & 2 in the University Centre)

Student Loans and Scholarships

A fully registered co-op work term is 4.5 credits and conveys full time student status.  Nevertheless, it is your responsibility to ensure that your co-op work term does not impact your student loans or scholarships, and that you promptly register any co-op work term extensions with the ECS co-op office in order to retain your student status and avoid financial consequences.

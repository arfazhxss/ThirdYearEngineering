Why? 

Engineering, computer science, and information technology (IT) are more than math, science and programming. Those who work in these fields have a profound impact on society and in turn, our ever-evolving society impacts how this work is carried out. Successful engineers, computer scientists, and those working in IT will need to have an understanding of how their respective fields interact with the economy and the environment and with those who do not work in these areas.

This course provides instruction at an opportune time in your engineering or computer science education: your first co-op work term. As you progress through the units of this course, take the opportunity to apply and validate the skills you gain in the course as you work in your current position.
